# Quick Download Guide - Ambient Nature Sounds

## ✅ Corrected Sources (Nature Sounds, Not Music!)

### 1. Pixabay Sound Effects ⭐ RECOMMENDED
**URL:** https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/

**Why:** Free, no account needed, CC0 license (no attribution), has actual nature sounds

**How to use:**
1. Visit the URL above
2. Search for each sound type (see below)
3. Make sure you're in "Sound Effects" section (not "Music")
4. Download MP3 files
5. Rename to match our convention

### 2. Freesound.org ⭐ BEST QUALITY
**URL:** https://freesound.org/

**Why:** Highest quality, huge library, CC0 sounds available

**How to use:**
1. Create free account (takes 2 minutes)
2. Search for sounds
3. **Filter by CC0 license** (no attribution needed)
4. Download files
5. Rename to match our convention

## Quick Search Terms

### Ocean Waves
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/ocean%20waves/
- Freesound: "ocean waves loop" (filter: CC0)

### Rain
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/rain/
- Freesound: "rain loop" (filter: CC0)

### Forest
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/forest/
- Freesound: "forest ambience" (filter: CC0)

### Zen/Meditation
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/meditation/
- Freesound: "meditation ambient" (filter: CC0)

### Noise
- Freesound: "white noise loop" (filter: CC0)
- Or generate online

## File Requirements

- ✅ Format: MP3
- ✅ Duration: 1-5 minutes (longer = better for looping)
- ✅ Type: Sound Effects/Ambient (NOT music tracks)
- ✅ License: CC0 or Free for commercial use
- ✅ Quality: 44.1kHz, 128-192 kbps

## Naming After Download

Rename files exactly as:
- `ambient_ocean.mp3`
- `ambient_rain.mp3`
- `ambient_forest.mp3`
- `ambient_zen.mp3`
- `ambient_noise_white.mp3`

(Full list in DOWNLOAD_INSTRUCTIONS.md)
